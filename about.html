Hey, I'm Sam

My Background

I Started This Because I Love Car Culture.

I've always been around cars, and picking up a camera made me see them differently. Behind the Lens started as a passion project so I could document cars the way I see them in real life.


I like simple, honest photos that show shape, stance and detail without overdoing it. Most of my shoots are one-on-one and relaxed. We choose a location, chase good light, and build the shoot from there.


If you've got a car you're proud of, I'd love to help you capture it properly.
